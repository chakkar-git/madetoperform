Choose Your Group

Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or ready to take things to the next level, there's a program tailored for you.

🔹 Group 1

Fundamentals (Beginners)

Perfect for women who are new to training or returning after a break. You'll build strength, confidence, and understanding — inside and outside the gym.

Focus Areas:

  • Learning proper form and technique
  • Building consistency with strength and cardio
  • Understanding nutrition basics and macros
  • Creating a healthy, balanced routine

You'll finish the six weeks feeling stronger, more in control, and ready to keep going.

🔸 Group 2

Perform (Experienced)

For women who have been training consistently and are ready to level up.

Focus Areas:

  • Advanced progressive strength training
  • Lifting heavier, improving performance
  • Deep dive into advanced nutrition
  • Optimising training for body composition

This group is all about elevating your results and learning how to perform — not just train.
